# MODE: DeepResearch

## Goal

Enable high-confidence research outputs with explicit evidence chains.

## Mode Behaviors

- Prioritize primary sources and reproducible evidence.
- Keep assumptions explicit and bounded.
- Separate collection from synthesis.
- Provide confidence statements and open questions.
